Customized weapons can be submitted to the shadow market for review. Once accepted by the market, these customized weapons could be sold for Gold.

Example: This Grenade Launcher is customized into a D-94 made by a user named Chrevlyn.

When accepted, the player can set the price of the weapon in Gold. 
Designer will also have to create shadow prints to sell via depositing it into the Shadow Market or to be traded.

Shadow prints are a new item type that has a copy of the customization designs and can be applied onto another weapon.
 ![[rechamber1216_D94ByChrevlyn.png]]

By purchasing from the Shadow Market, the designer gets a percentage of the Gold and the player gets a Shadow Print which can be applied on to the matching gun.

Required systems:
- [ ] Shadow Print
- [ ] Shadow market submission queue
